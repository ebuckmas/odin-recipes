Peanut Butter and Jelly Milkshake

Description

I love chocolate milkshakes. I love peanut butter and jelly sandwhiches. This recipe is the perfect combo!

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons peanut butter
  • 3 tablespoons grape jelly
  • 1 cup milk
  • 5 cubes ice
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Stir peanut butter and jelly together in a bowl until smooth.
  2. Blend milk, ice, and vanilla extract in a blender for 30 seconds; add the peanut butter mixture and continue blending until smooth, about 30 seconds more.
